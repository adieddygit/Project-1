Health services access can be overwhelming especially when people need specialty/advance health care in a facility without any thorough information about the kind of services available and the appropriate time to visit.

At PHFAS, we make health care access easy and convenient at your finger tip. We help you avoid all the stress and hassles and make informed decisions concerning your health.

Our Services are Tailored to your Needs with our dedicated staff online 24/7 ready to offer all the necessary assistance.

  • You are able to know the kind of services a health facility offers which suits your health needs.

  • You will be able to make informed choices and book appointments online without having to travel to a health facility first.

  • As a user, you get informed whether a service is covered under the available Health Insurance Scheme and the cost if services rendered is cash and carry.

  • Users can also access their clinical history and information online about their previous visits to a health facility and be reminded of review appointments.

  • Health providers can make the necessary provisions by referring to the system and organize well to provide optimal health care services to clients.

HEALTH 360

Health 360 is a holistic approach to health and well-being that considers all aspects of an individual's life. Instead of focusing solely on physical health, it takes into account various dimensions, including mental, emotional, social, and even spiritual aspects. The idea is to address health in a comprehensive and integrated manner, recognizing that these different dimensions are interconnected and can impact each other.
By 360-degree approach, individuals aim to create a balanced and sustainable lifestyle that promotes overall health and well-being. This perspective emphasizes that health is not just the absence of illness but involves actively cultivating a state of flourishing across various dimensions of life.
