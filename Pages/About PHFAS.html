Health services access can be overwhelming especially
when people need specialty/advance health care in a facility without any
thorough information about the kind of services available and the appropriate time to visit.

Here at PHFAS, we make health care access easy and convenient at your finger tip.
We help you avoid all the stress and hassles and informed decisions concerning your health.

  • You are able to know the kind of services a health
    facility offers which suits your health needs.

  • You will be able to make informed choices and book
    appointments online without having to travel to a health facility first.

  • As a user, will be informed whether a service is covered
    under the available Health Insurance Scheme and the cost if services rendered is cash and carry.

  • Users can also access their clinical history and information
    online about their previous visits to a health facility and be reminded of review appointments.

  • Health providers can make the necessary provisions by referring
    to the system and organize well to provide optimal health care services to clients.
